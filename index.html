Übertown, Nowhere’s largest, most kick-ass, party space is back again. Building on the success of our highly impressive theatrical dj stage set-piece at London Decompression, we’re pulling out all the stops and are, once again, setting a new bar for the level of detail and art-working in Barrio design and decor.

We’ve major plans for a new and improved DJ stage, building on the narrative of Übertown - a post-apocalyptic, failed Soviet state. Think Mad Max meets Karl Marx - Mad Marx, if you will.

We don’t want to give the game away too much, it’d spoil the surprise, but one aspect we’re really keen to introduce is FIRE!

This year, excluding food and water for our camp mates, we will spend approximately £8,500 on building our camp and giving away booze at our bar for all you Nobodies. While that money is enough to provide an awesome sound and light spectacle in a richly themed and immersive party space, it falls short of the money we need to accomplish our creative ambitions.

As a Barrio, we’re ineligible for Art Grant support, nevertheless we’ve always strived to create a camp full of creative content, to push expectations of what can be achieved and to promote healthy competition between Barrios.

We understand that the Nowhere community is all about helping each other make the event the best it can be, that’s why we implement a power grid to supply a number of Barrios and assist Barrios, Art, NOrg and individuals in transporting stuff to site from London with the contracting of a road freight company.

So we put it to you, the Nowhere/Burner community, to help get this fire started.

It’s important to us that you get something for your contribution. So we’re trying something new for a crowd funding campaign and we're giving you something you can actually use at a price that doesn’t take the piss! So we’ve designed a load of Übertown swag as perks.

We are speccing the fire puffers to UK requirements and are taking every care to ensure their safety, as elsewhere in our camp. please take a look at our 2014 Health and safety statement.
